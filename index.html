Rotuma Day Celebration in Utah

West Valley City, Utah

You and your family are invited to a day of joyous and festive celebration of the Rotuman people with games, music, food and family connection. Rotuma Day is an annual celebration on the island of Rotuma, a Fijian dependency. It falls on May 13, the anniversary of the island's cession to the United Kingdom in 1881.

Time: 3:45 p.m.–9:00 p.m.
Location: 3650 W 4400 S, West Valley City, UT 84120
